Examples of using GUPPY

Genetic Understanding Perspective Preview sYstem

These examples of guppy script illustrate how to use GUPPY for file conversions to generate sequence maps. Since they are a template for various kind of data format, some improvements may be nessesary in practical use. Although they are publically available through the internet, users are reminded to present credits to original works and follow their conditions for the use. We appreciate their permissions to use their data.
